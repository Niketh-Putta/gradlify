Common questions

Do grade boundaries change each year?

Yes. Boundaries are set after marking based on paper difficulty. They can vary significantly from year to year, so use these as a guide rather than a guarantee.

Higher vs Foundation — what's the difference?

Higher tier covers grades 4–9, Foundation covers grades 1–5. The papers have different questions and the boundaries are set separately. Your teacher will advise which tier suits you best.

Is this accurate for AQA / Edexcel / OCR?

We include real boundaries for all major GCSE and IGCSE boards. Select your board from the dropdown to see the correct thresholds. Each board sets its own boundaries independently.

Why is my grade only an estimate?

Official boundaries are only finalised after exams are marked each year. We use the most recent published data to give you a realistic target, but your actual grade may differ slightly.
